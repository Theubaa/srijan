Admission Process

  1. Submit an online inquiry or visit our academy.
  2. Attend a counseling session to discuss goals and batch options.
  3. Take a short assessment (if applicable) for appropriate level.
  4. Complete registration and submit documents.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to students of Classes 3–12 from any recognized school/board.
  • Willingness to attend regular classes and participate in assessments.

Required Documents

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • School ID or last mark sheet (copy)
  • Parent/Guardian contact details

For detailed fee information, please contact us. We offer flexible payment options.
